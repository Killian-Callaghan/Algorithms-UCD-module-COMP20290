The output of encoding is saved in "EncodedOutput.txt"
EncodedOutput.txt is then used to decode.
The output of decoding is saved in "DecodedOutput.txt"

Console commands:
Press '-' to encode,
Press '+' to decode
and '0' to end the programme.
